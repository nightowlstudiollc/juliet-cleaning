Services

We offer a range of cleaning services for residential properties, from routine maintenance to deep cleaning for special occasions.

Standard House Cleaning

Regular maintenance cleaning for homes. Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom and kitchen cleaning.

Deep Cleaning

Intensive cleaning for move-ins, move-outs, or when your home needs extra attention. Includes appliances, baseboards, and hard-to-reach areas.

Floor Care

Hardwood floor cleaning and polishing, tile and grout cleaning, carpet cleaning (steam or dry methods).

Window Cleaning

Interior window cleaning to let the light in. Includes tracks and sills.

Upholstery & Furniture

Cleaning and refreshing of upholstered furniture, including couches, chairs, and cushions.

Organizing & Special Projects

Garage, basement, or attic clean-outs. Home organizing. Specialty cleaning projects like silverware polishing or collectibles dusting.

About

We've been cleaning homes in the Inland Northwest—Spokane, Spokane Valley, Post Falls, and Coeur d'Alene—since 2021. Our approach is simple: thorough work, attention to detail, and treating your home with care.

As a small, local business, we build long-term relationships with our clients. Many of our customers have been with us for years, trusting us with weekly or bi-weekly cleanings. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and make sure you're happy with the results.

We work with all property types—apartments, condos, single-family homes, and multi-unit buildings. Whether you need regular maintenance cleaning or a one-time deep clean before a move or special event, we're here to help.
