Who We Are

Having served the Inland Northwest since 2021, we here at T&J Cleaning are experienced in providing thorough, quality cleanings that ensure that you come home to a refreshing, reset environment.

We specialize in residential cleanings, making it our mission to alleviate the stresses of the everyday hustle and bustle by making the scrubbing and scouring one less thing on your to-do list. Whether there just aren't enough hours in the day or you'd like a professional shine to your living space, you need someone that you not only can trust, but someone that you can trust will do the job properly. Have pets? No stress. Kids to keep up with? Consider it taken care of.

As a small, local business, we take immense pride in cultivating long-lasting relationships with our clients, many of whom have been with us since the very beginning, entrusting us with their weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleanings.

We work with all property types—apartments, condos, single-family homes, and multi-unit buildings. Whether you need regularly scheduled maintenance cleanings or a one-time deep clean before a special event, we're here to help.
